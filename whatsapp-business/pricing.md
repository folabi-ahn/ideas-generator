# Pricing Strategy — WhatsApp Business Automation

## Recommended Package Structure

### Starter — €1,200 setup + €299/month
**Best for:** Hair salons, small restaurants, boutiques
- WhatsApp Business API connection
- 1 automated flow (e.g., appointment reminder OR welcome message)
- Broadcast list setup (up to 500 contacts)
- 30-day post-setup support
- Monthly: 2 broadcast campaigns, basic analytics report

### Growth — €1,800 setup + €449/month
**Best for:** Busy restaurants, clinics, mid-size retailers
- Everything in Starter
- 3 automated flows (booking, reminder, follow-up)
- CRM integration (Google Sheets or Airtable)
- Monthly: 4 broadcast campaigns, performance call
- Response-time SLA (flows respond 24/7)

### Pro — €2,500 setup + €649/month
**Best for:** Real estate agencies, multi-location businesses, clinics
- Everything in Growth
- Unlimited automated flows
- Full CRM integration (their existing tool or Airtable setup)
- AI-assisted auto-replies for common questions
- Monthly: unlimited broadcasts, weekly analytics, dedicated WhatsApp
- Priority support

## Why Setup Fee Matters

Setup fees solve your cashflow problem in the early months while retainer base builds. Don't skip it:
- Covers your time (4–8 hours per setup)
- Filters out tire-kickers (only serious buyers pay setup)
- Creates psychological commitment from the client
- You make money on day 1, not month 4

## Competitive Pricing Anchor

Show prospects what the DIY path costs:
- WATI subscription: €59–€299/month (they still have to set it up)
- Hiring a social media manager to handle WhatsApp: €800–€1,500/month
- Your service: done for them, cheaper than staff, smarter than DIY

**Positioning:** "Less than one part-time employee. Zero setup headache. Up and running in 5 days."
