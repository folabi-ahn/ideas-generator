## Cultural Playbook

### What Builds Trust
- **Being Albanian/from Kosovo yourself** — massive trust accelerator
- **Personal connections** — introduction through someone they know
- **Community endorsement** — if Swissalbs or RRBDSH backs you, trust follows
- **Demonstrating results** — show real numbers from other Albanian business owners

### What Kills Trust
- Cold corporate emails in German/English
- Rushing the sale — relationship comes before business
- Generic marketing — they want to know you're "one of us"

### Language Strategy
- **Sell in Albanian** → your conversations, onboarding, support
- **Automate in German** → the chatbot their German-speaking customers interact with
- In Austria: 62% use Albanian as main language, 34% use both

### Buying Behavior
- Referral-driven — if one restaurant owner adopts, others in the community follow
- Relationship over transaction — patience required
- Reputation matters more than marketing spend
