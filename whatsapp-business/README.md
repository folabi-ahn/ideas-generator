# WhatsApp Business Automation — Project Workspace

**Concept:** Done-for-you WhatsApp Business API setup + ongoing management for European local businesses.

**Target Revenue:** €5,000–€10,000/month
**Validation Status:** ✅ VIABLE (from pipeline research)

---

## The Opportunity in One Sentence

> 80%+ of European SMBs use WhatsApp to talk to customers, but less than 10% use any automation — leaving a massive, daily operational pain completely unaddressed by existing software-only tools.

## Business Model

```
Setup (one-time):   €1,200–€2,000
Monthly retainer:   €300–€500/month
```

**Revenue math:**
- Month 1: 3 clients = €4,500–€6,000 setup fees alone
- Month 3: 15 clients on retainer = €4,500–€7,500/month recurring
- Month 6: 20 clients = €6,000–€10,000/month recurring

## Target Verticals (prioritized)

1. **Restaurants** — booking confirmations, daily specials, table reminders
2. **Hair salons / Beauty clinics** — appointment booking, reminders, re-booking
3. **Real estate agencies** — property alerts, viewing confirmations, follow-ups
4. **Medical/dental clinics** — appointment confirmations, post-visit follow-ups
5. **Local retailers** — promotions, back-in-stock alerts, loyalty

---

## Competitors (and the gap)

| Player | What they do | What they miss |
|--------|-------------|----------------|
| WATI | SaaS platform, self-serve | No setup help, no hand-holding |
| Landbot | Chatbot builder | Complex, no local business focus |
| Chatarmin | Germany-focused SaaS | Software only, not a service |
| Respond.io | Enterprise CRM | Too expensive for SMBs |

**The gap:** Nobody is doing the *service* layer — setup, configuration, ongoing management — for local businesses that can't DIY.
