# Go-to-Market — WhatsApp Business Automation

## Geography to Start: Spain

**Why Spain first:**
- 92% WhatsApp penetration — highest in Europe
- Huge restaurant/hospitality sector, all using WhatsApp manually
- Warm business culture — phone/WhatsApp outreach is normal and accepted
- Less saturated than Germany (Chatarmin has Germany somewhat covered)
- Easy to expand to Portugal, LatAm with same language

---

## First 10 Clients — Exact Playbook

### Week 1: Warm Network
1. Message every business owner you know personally
2. Offer a **free WhatsApp audit** ("I'll tell you exactly what you're missing and how to fix it — 20 min call, no pitch")
3. At the end of the audit call, present the Starter package
4. Ask for 1 referral regardless of whether they buy

**Target: 1–2 clients from network**

### Week 2–3: Local Outreach (Walk-in or WhatsApp)
Pick one vertical first (restaurants work best for demo purposes):
1. Google Maps: search "restaurants [your city]" — collect 50 names + WhatsApp numbers from their Google Business profile
2. Message them on WhatsApp (ironic, but effective):

> "Hi [Name], I saw [Restaurant Name] on Google Maps. I help restaurants in [City] set up automatic WhatsApp replies so they never miss a booking, even at 2am. Takes 5 days to set up, and I handle everything. Would you be open to a quick 15-min chat this week?"

3. Follow up once after 3 days if no reply
4. Book 10 calls → expect 3–5 to show → close 1–2

**Target: 2–3 clients from cold outreach**

### Week 4+: Content + Referrals
1. Post a LinkedIn case study after first client setup: "How I helped [Restaurant] in [City] stop missing WhatsApp bookings at night"
2. Ask each client for 2 referrals (incentivize: €100 off one month)
3. Join local business Facebook Groups (every city has them) and share the case study
4. Reach out to business associations, chambers of commerce — offer to present at their next meetup

## Demo That Closes Deals

The most powerful sales tool: a live demo.

Set up a demo WhatsApp number. When on a call with a prospect, say:

> "Here, message our demo number right now."

They send "Hi" and immediately receive:
- An auto-reply in their language
- A menu of options (Book a table / Ask a question / See today's specials)
- A follow-up asking for their name

They see it in real time. **That closes more deals than any deck.**

Cost to build: 2 hours + €0 (use free tier).

## Sales Script (cold outreach call)

**Opening:**
"Thanks for taking my call. Quick question — when customers message you on WhatsApp outside business hours, what happens?"

*(They say: nothing, or I try to reply when I can)*

"Right. That's the problem I solve. I set up automatic responses so the customer gets an instant reply at 2am, you wake up to a confirmed booking. Takes 5 days, I handle everything. Want me to show you how it works for a restaurant like yours?"

**Handling "we already have WhatsApp Business":**
"Totally — most of my clients did too. The difference is automations. WhatsApp Business app can do basic away messages. What I set up is full chatbot flows, broadcast campaigns to your entire customer list, and CRM integration so you know who your regulars are. Want to see what that looks like?"

**Closing:**
"The setup is a one-time €1,200. After that it's €299/month — less than one day of a part-time employee. You'd be up and running in 5 days. Want to get started this week?"

## Referral Program

After client is live and happy (day 30):

> "I'm growing through referrals — I don't do paid ads. If you refer another business owner who becomes a client, I'll take €100 off your next month. Do you know anyone who might benefit from this?"

Most business owners know 5-10 other business owners. One happy restaurant client can unlock an entire network of local businesses.
